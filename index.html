Always open to exploring new opportunities. If you come across a role that you believe aligns with my expertise and interests, I'd be happy to discuss it further. Feel free to reach out to me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn at your convenience.

  • Frontend development utilizing React, JavaScript, Sass, and React Storybook
  • Backend development using Java and Spring Boot

  • Cloud-based work with Google Cloud Platform and its services such as BigQuery, Kubernetes Engine, Cloud Storage, Cron Job, and more.
What is your work authorization status? I am a US citizen and do not require sponsorship of any kind, now or in the future. How soon would you be available to start in a new role? In line with professional etiquette, I would tender my two-week notice to my current employer on the same day that I sign a new employment contract. Following the completion of this fourteen-day period, I would promptly commence my duties in the new role. On-site, hybrid, or remote? Remote would be my preferred option. I have successfully worked remotely for IKEA since August 2020 and am accustomed to collaborating and operating in a digital environment. Despite residing on the west coast, my work schedule is aligned with the eastern time zone, and I’m typically online from 6:30am to 2:30pm PST. Essentially, I am highly adaptable when it comes to my working hours and can adjust them to accommodate the team's needs. I am also open to occasional travel for significant meetings and milestones, as this has been a regular practice in my current role with IKEA.  What is your desired salary? My target base salary is $150,000 - $250,000, which is based on my experience, the current figures I’m seeing for software engineers in the Los Angeles area, insights gathered from recruiters and colleagues, and the high cost of living in Southern California. However, I am flexible regarding this figure, particularly if the compensation package includes stock options and an annual bonus. While I value fair compensation, my priority is joining a great company with an exceptional team and engaging projects that allow me to expand my knowledge base.
