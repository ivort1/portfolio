About me
My coding journey began with customizing my MySpace page using HTML and CSS. Originally from Southern California, I made the decision to venture out of state for college and attended St. John's University in Queens, NY. There, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2015.
After graduating, I joined IKEA and have been working as a software engineer since 2020. My primary focus is on the frontend development of my team's projects, although I also explore other areas as needed. I thoroughly enjoy the challenges and creativity that comes with building user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces.
If you're in need of a personal or business website, I'm here to help. Feel free to reach out through email and together, we can explore the best options that suit your needs.
Recruiters interested in discussing career opportunities are also welcome to get in touch. Feel free to reach out to me directly or connect with me on LinkedIn. Don't forget to grab a copy of my resume to learn more about my qualifications and experience.
